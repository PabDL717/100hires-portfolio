## Post 1
**URL:** https://www.linkedin.com/posts/edward-sturm_social-media-is-now-a-battleground-for-review-activity-7470931746340085762-lDe6
**Date:** ~June 2026

Social media is now a battleground for review content on your brand. The most-clicked websites in search are social media. Instagram, Facebook, TikTok, X, LinkedIn, YouTube.

There is a ridiculous opportunity for brands that are collecting video testimonials, dropping them on social media, starting the post with this language specifically: "your brand name," then the word "review."

Here's what happens when you do this a lot. A searcher trying to figure out if your brand is credible Googles reviews for you. All of Google is showing these reviews that you put up across all social media. Another searcher is asking ChatGPT. ChatGPT goes and searches for reviews of your brand, sees all of the reviews that you put up.

Make sure to put a transcript of your reviews with the description when you post it. This way AI can read it.
