Google's AI Overviews are one of the most visible ways AI appears in search results today, but they're continually changing. With the introduction of AI Mode, it's clear Google is testing how and when AI answers appear, especially in regulated industries like finance.

Between April and September 2025, we tracked the rise and fall of AI Overviews presence for finance sectors and the results were... interesting.

For marketing leaders, the main takeaway is that visibility isn't guaranteed. Having a strategy for how your brand appears in AI search results will help you stay visible as these changes continue.
