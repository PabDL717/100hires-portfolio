When creating pages for SEO / AEO... They both work when you have the right ingredients. If you slop it up, then your results will be temporary.

Some ways to elevate your AI content:

1. Build your search brainiac
- Data layer: GSC, GA4, AI search / rank tracking
- Company layer: products/services, FAQ, etc.
- Expertise / experience layer: founder / SME interviews, UGC, etc.

2. Create swipe file
- Study your top competitors
- Convert their best content into a template
- Repeat for ~100 assets

3. Research the right way
- Identify top competitors for your target keyword from traditional search + AI citations
- Extract entities + content structure

4. Perform a page-level gap analysis
- Throw the top competitors into ChatGPT and ask it to identify topic gaps

5. Build your purple cow content strategy
- You're equipped with what works, what's relevant, and the gaps. Now create a content angle.

6. Create a killer content brief
- Intelligence from your search brainiac
- At least 3 proven templates that match the content type
- Most important entities to cover
- Purple cow differentiation

It's never been easier to win in search. Just take a look at most SERPs and AI citation sets. They're polluted with AI slop with no differentiation whatsoever.

All it takes is 10% more effort and you'll smoke most competitors. A perfect example is below for the keyword "best SEO books." I've ranked for this keyword for years, and I haven't touched this content once.

One of the key differentiators right now, as far as dominating your competition in search, is simply effort. Everyone is looking for the quickest way to do things, the most automated way to do things, and the things that require the least amount of effort.

The pendulum will swing back to the other end, where putting in more effort will help you stand out, especially in a sea of people who have no interest in putting in any extra effort.
