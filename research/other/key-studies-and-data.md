# Key Studies and Data Points — AI-Powered SEO Content Production

A collection of studies, reports, and data points referenced by the experts in this research. All data points are sourced directly from the expert content collected in this repository.

---

## 1. Ahrefs Q1 2026 AI Search Benchmark
**Cited by:** Ahrefs (LinkedIn post, May 2026)
**Source:** Ahrefs internal research
**Key findings:**
- AI Mode and AI Overviews agree 86% of the time, but their sources overlap only 13.7%
- YouTube mentions beat backlinks, DR, and branded search as a predictor of AI visibility
- Only 28% of brand mentions in AI responses include a link back
- 38% of pages cited in AI Overviews still rank in the top 10
- Built on 146M SERPs, 730K AI responses, and 75K brands

## 2. Ahrefs AI Overviews Click Impact Study
**Cited by:** Ahrefs (YouTube, April 2026), Eric Siu (YouTube, October 2025)
**Source:** Ahrefs internal research
**Key findings:**
- When an AI overview appears in search results, the #1 ranking page loses approximately 35% of its clicks
- Updated study shows the loss has grown to 58%

## 3. Ahrefs AI Overview Citation Analysis
**Cited by:** Ahrefs (YouTube, April 2026)
**Source:** Ahrefs internal research
**Key findings:**
- 86% of top cited sources are unique to each AI assistant
- Google AI Overviews lean heavily on YouTube, Reddit, and Quora
- ChatGPT prefers publishers and news outlets
- Perplexity tends to cite niche and regional sites
- Branded mentions have the strongest correlation with AI Overview visibility — stronger than backlinks, referring domains, and domain rating

---

## 4. Ahrefs Content Length vs AI Citation Study
**Cited by:** Ahrefs (YouTube, May 2026)
**Source:** Ahrefs internal research — 174,000+ pages analyzed
**Key findings:**
- Correlation between word count and getting cited in AI is just 0.04 (essentially zero)
- 53.4% of all pages cited in AI Overviews are under 1,000 words
- Content cited by AI is on average 25.7% fresher than what ranks in traditional organic results
- 89.7% of ChatGPT's top cited pages were updated in 2025
- 76% of ChatGPT's top cited pages were refreshed within the last 30 days
- 43.8% of all pages cited by ChatGPT are listicles (best X, top X, comparisons, reviews)

## 5. Surfer AI Overview Study
**Cited by:** Surfer Academy (YouTube, June 2025)
**Source:** Surfer internal research — 400,000+ searches analyzed
**Key findings:**

## 7. Neil Patel / NP Digital GEO Revenue Study
**Cited by:** Neil Patel (LinkedIn, June 2026; YouTube, November 2025)
**Source:** NP Digital internal client data — 100 businesses analyzed
**Key findings:**
- As GEO visibility increased, profit from that channel also increased
- AI traffic is less than 1% of total site visits but already drives over 5% of sales
- Customers referred through AI have a lifetime value of $325 vs $271 for Google traffic
- 41% of AI-driven conversions never show up in analytics (users go directly to the site)
- In B2B companies, AI platforms responsible for 9.7% of revenue
- In B2C companies, AI platforms responsible for 11.4% of revenue
- ChatGPT drove roughly 5.8% of total online sales for companies investing in GEO

## 8. NP Digital AI Overview Click Impact Study
**Cited by:** Neil Patel (YouTube, May 2026)
**Source:** NP Digital internal research — ~1,000 keywords analyzed
**Key findings:**
- Click-through rates dropped significantly on queries where AI Overviews appeared
- AI Overviews reduce click-through rates by more than 50% on affected paid search queries
- When analyzing 48 sites: organic traffic was dropping but direct revenue was going up
- Well over half of Google searches now end without a website click
